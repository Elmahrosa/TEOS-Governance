# Governance Roles

## Citizen
- Must sign the civic petition  
- Can view treasury and submit proposals  
- Cannot initiate flows or approve updates

## Validator
- Must hold Validator badge  
- Can verify flows, approve updates, assign badges  
- Logs all actions in `chapter_resurrection.md`

## Founder
- Must hold Founder badge  
- Can initiate flows, inject assets, ratify chapters  
- Responsible for compliance and treasury integrity
